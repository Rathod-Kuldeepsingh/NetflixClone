Hello I'm John Wick

John Wick is a legendary assassin who comes out of retirement after a group of criminals kills his puppy — a final gift from his late wife. Known as the “Baba Yaga,” he is feared in the underworld for his ruthless efficiency and unmatched combat skills. As he seeks revenge, he’s drawn back into a world of assassins, secret rules, and high-stakes violence. The story unfolds with intense action, loyalty, and a code of honor.
